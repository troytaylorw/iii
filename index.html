INTERSECTIONAL INTERFACE INDEX
This an index of current designers and artists who are rethinking the interface and digital space. This site intends to center people of color, particularly queer, femme voices. This database takes inspiration from Polymode’s Queer History Timeline, Mindy Seu’s Cyberfeminism Index, and Working Not Working’s Where Are The Black Designers. By focusing on web designers and online practitioners, the hope for this space is to act as a living group show and community of radical thinkers.
Legacy Russell
Legacy Russell is a curator, writer, and artist whose creative work focuses on gender, performance, digital selfdom, internet idolatry, and new media ritual. Her book, Glitch Feminism is a vital new chapter in cyberfeminism, one that explores the relationship between gender, technology and identity.

Annika Hansteen-Izora
Annika is an art director, designer and poet exploring joy, dreaming, & digital worlds. They're currently the creative director of design and UI for Somewhere Good, Ethel's Club, and Form No Form.

Vanessa Newman
Vanessa is a strategist, designer, writer, music producer and DJ whose expertise lies in identifying gaps within people of color and queer communities where needs aren’t being met and opportunities aren’t being tapped and creating successful physical, digital and product spaces that don’t exist within those margins.

Vanessa is working on Somewhere Good
Taeyoon Choi
Taeyoon Choi is a contemporary artist who works with drawing, painting, computer programming, performance art and video. He cofounded the School for Poetic Computation in New York. He lives in Seoul, South Korea.
